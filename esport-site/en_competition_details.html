Game Summary

Elden Ring is an action-RPG (Role Playing Game) developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, released on February 25, 2022. Directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, with a contribution to the world-building by writer George R. R. Martin, the game immerses players in the Forbidden Lands, an open world rich in myths and dangers.

The concept is based on free exploration of a vast world filled with hostile creatures, hidden secrets, and formidable bosses. The player controls a Tarnished, tasked with restoring the Elden Ring and becoming the Elden Lord by overcoming many challenges, while customizing their playstyle with various weapons, magic, and skills.

The Olympic Event

For this event, several categories are possible for competitors:

  • The Any% Glitchless mode, which requires players not to use game glitches that would make it easier, gives the player the opportunity to use all the resources provided by the game to defeat a series of bosses as quickly as possible, excluding game bugs.
  • The second mode involves defeating a series of bosses using Rune L1, meaning players must not upgrade their abilities. After defeating the bosses, competitors must limit themselves to a single item from the boss, and can only use the DLC equipment, so no extras allowed.

Players will therefore be evaluated based on the time it takes to defeat a major enemy, and the players with the best times will progress to the next stage.

Game Summary

F1 28 is a racing game, developed by Codemasters and published by EA Sports in June 2027. It is the 21st installment in the Formula One game series, allowing players to step into the shoes of a real F1 driver. It features several functionalities, including online player connectivity, which is essential for the event awaiting players.

The Olympic Event

In this event, players will compete like a real Formula 1 race. Each pair of players belongs to a team and competes against each other. In a 20-player ranking, only the top ten score points. To determine the starting order for the race, players participate in a qualifying race where the player with the best lap time secures pole position, the first place.

Game Summary

Fortnite is a video game developed by Epic Games, released in July 2017. It combines several game modes, but is primarily known for its Battle Royale mode, where 100 players fight on an island to be the last survivor. This mode includes unique building mechanics that allow players to construct structures to defend, attack, or explore.

The game is distinguished by its colorful graphical style, dynamic events (such as in-game concerts or collaborations with famous franchises), and its business model based on microtransactions for purely cosmetic items. Fortnite has become a cultural phenomenon thanks to its accessibility, regular updates, and its role in the evolution of online multiplayer games.

The Olympic Event

The Fortnite event consists of a battle royale match, where teams of three players compete for victory on a map. Once deployed, players must find resources (whether weapons or materials) on the map in order to attack and defend against enemies effectively.

Game Summary

Hearthstone is an online collectible card game developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment, released in March 2014. Inspired by the Warcraft universe, the game is accessible, strategic, and involves duels between players (or against AI).

Each player controls a hero with specific powers, builds a deck of cards (creatures, spells, weapons, etc.), and must reduce the opponent's health points to zero. Hearthstone stands out with its vibrant design, simple-to-understand but hard-to-master mechanics, and its evolving format with regular expansions added to refresh strategies and the meta-game.

The Olympic Event

The event involves two players facing off. The best player qualifies after five matches. In the semifinals, the winner of the match is determined by the winner of three duels. Each player will be provided with a standard deck of cards for the event.

Hollow Knight is a Metroidvania action-adventure game developed and published by Team Cherry, first released in February 2017. The game features exploration, platforming, and combat in a beautifully designed 2D world.

Game Summary

Mario Kart is a famous racing video game series developed and published by Nintendo, starting with Super Mario Kart in 1992 on the Super Nintendo. This multiplayer game features characters from the Mario universe racing on various tracks using custom karts.

The concept revolves around fun and dynamic races where players can use items (such as shells, bananas, and mushrooms) to attack, defend, or boost their speed. The series is known for its accessibility, competitive nature, and various game modes, including tournaments, arena battles, and online races. Mario Kart has become a staple of game nights and friendly competitions, loved for its mix of strategy and chaotic fun.

The Olympic Event

The Mario Kart event will feature two teams of 6 players racing in the 200cc version, the fastest mode of the game. They will compete across four cups (Mushroom, Star, Green Shell, etc.), each consisting of four races. The team with the most points will advance to the next stage.

The semi-finals and final will consist of four races, and the tracks will be chosen either by public vote (via Twitch) or by the judges.

Public vote: The idea is that the judges will choose the cup in which the track will be, and a poll will be held on Twitch with the available tracks for that cup. The number of votes will decide the track for each race.

Game Summary

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios and first released in 2011. A sandbox game genre typically lacks a specific goal, allowing the player to evolve freely. Minecraft became a global phenomenon thanks to its gameplay based on creativity, exploration, and survival.

The player navigates an open world made of blocks that they can destroy, collect, and use to build structures or craft tools and items. Two main gameplay modes dominate:

  • Survival: The player must manage resources, avoid dangers like monsters, and meet their needs.
  • Creative: The player has unlimited resources to let their imagination run wild.

With its iconic pixel art style and active community, Minecraft has become a tool for expression and learning, continuing to evolve through regular updates and mods. A mod is a player-created modification that adds extra content to the game.

The Olympic Event

During the Minecraft event, competitors are given a theme and must create something related to that theme within a set time limit. Each new phase features increasingly complex themes. Competitors will be evaluated based on the time spent creating their piece, creativity, and complexity.

Game Summary

Overcooked 2 is a cooperative cooking simulation game developed by Ghost Town Games and published by Team17. Released in August 2018, it is the sequel to the popular Overcooked. In this game, players take on the role of chefs who must collaborate to prepare, cook, and serve various dishes in quirky and sometimes chaotic kitchens.

The game is distinguished by its creative and wacky levels, with kitchens located in improbable places like hot air balloons, moving trains, or magical worlds. The mechanics include extra challenges, such as conveyor belts, moving platforms, and unexpected obstacles.

Overcooked 2 introduces new features, including the ability to throw ingredients, an online mode, and new recipes. It is a game that tests the players' coordination and communication while providing a hilarious and friendly experience, perfect for playing with friends or family.

The Olympic Event

The Overcooked 2 event pits two duos against each other in three matches, with the team winning after three confrontations. A final match will take place in case of a tie, with the same rules.

Game Summary

Overwatch is a team-based first-person shooter developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment in May 2016. This multiplayer game involves six-player teams where each player takes on the role of a hero with unique abilities, weapons, and roles (attack, defense, or support).

The objective varies depending on the game mode, such as capturing control points or escorting a payload across the map. Overwatch is distinguished by its futuristic universe, diverse and charismatic characters, and its gameplay, which combines shooting, strategy, and cooperation.

The game became very successful thanks to its carefully crafted art direction, accessibility, and role in e-sports. It also received numerous updates, with new heroes, maps, and game modes, which kept the community engaged. In 2022, Overwatch 2 was launched, adding new content while staying true to the original game's spirit.

The Olympic Event

The Overwatch event brings five-player teams face to face. The match winners are determined by the points earned during the match. The three matches will be played on three different maps. In case of a tie, a fourth match may be played to determine the winner. This process remains the same for the semi-finals and the final.

Game Overview

Rocket League is a video game developed by Psyonix, released in July 2015. A unique blend of soccer and car racing, it is played with cars equipped with boosters that can jump and fly to hit a giant ball and score goals.

The game is especially appreciated for its accessibility, simple mechanics that are easy to learn but hard to master, and its competitive nature. It offers several modes, ranging from casual matches to ranked multiplayer, as well as special events. Since becoming free-to-play in 2020, Rocket League has attracted a large community and has become a reference in e-sports.

Olympic Event

Rocket League matches last only five minutes, and the best team of three players will qualify after five matches, while for the finals or semifinals, the victorious team will be decided after three matches.

In case of a tie, a final match will be played to determine the winner.

Game Overview

Street Fighter 6 is a fighting game developed and published by Capcom, released on June 2, 2023. It is the latest installment in the famous Street Fighter franchise, which has revolutionized the fighting game genre since its debut in 1987.

The game introduces major new features, such as the Drive system, which offers new strategic options in combat, and a World Tour mode, where players explore an open world to customize their avatar and experience a solo adventure. Classic one-on-one battles remain at the core of the experience, with improved graphics and a diverse roster featuring iconic characters from the series (Ryu, Chun-Li) as well as new fighters.

Street Fighter 6 also stands out for its beginner-friendly approach thanks to simplified controls, while still offering depth for experienced competitors, increasing its appeal to e-sports enthusiasts.

Olympic Event

In case of a tie (on points), a final match will decide the winner.

In the individual event, the confrontation is between two players only.

Game Overview

Super Smash Bros. is a series of fighting video games created by Masahiro Sakurai and published by Nintendo, starting in 1999 on the Nintendo 64. Unlike traditional fighting games, this series stands out with combat where the objective is to knock your opponents out of the arena rather than depleting their health bar.

The game features iconic characters from Nintendo franchises (like Mario, Link, Pikachu) as well as guest characters from other video game universes, engaging in dynamic battles on interactive stages. Each installment offers various modes: solo, local or online multiplayer, and competitive tournaments.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, released in 2018 on Nintendo Switch, is the most recent and ambitious entry, with a massive roster of over 80 playable characters. The series is a staple of party gatherings and a key player in e-sports thanks to its depth and diverse gameplay.

Game Overview

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter (FPS) developed and published by Riot Games, officially released on June 2, 2020. It combines elements from classic competitive FPS games like Counter-Strike with special hero abilities, similar to games like Overwatch.

In Valorant, two teams of five players compete, alternating between attacking and defending roles. The main objective is to plant or defuse a "spike" (equivalent to a bomb), while using characters called agents, each with unique abilities that add a strategic and dynamic dimension to the game.

The game is praised for its precise gameplay, well-designed maps, and its growing e-sport scene. Free-to-play, Valorant attracts a large community thanks to regular updates and a progression system based on skins and cosmetic rewards.

Olympic Event

Similar to League of Legends, Valorant, before the start of the event, goes through a banning phase, not banning players, but banning maps. Two teams of five players will compete on one of the maps, and the winners will be decided by points and who defuses the opposing team's bomb first.
